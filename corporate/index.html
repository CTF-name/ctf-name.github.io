Add Your Group

Simple steps to track your community.

Step 1: Add the Bot
To begin, invite @webhookos_bot to your group, community, or channel. Simply open your Telegram, search for the bot, and use the "Add to Group" option. Whether it’s a private team chat, a public community, or a corporate channel, the bot integrates effortlessly. Once added, it will instantly recognize its new environment and prepare to assist you with tracking and management tasks.

Step 2: Auto-Registration
After adding the bot, it automatically registers your group in the "Groups" section of its interface. No manual setup is needed @webhookos_bot detects the group’s details, such as its name, type (public or private), and member count. This step ensures that your corporate community is immediately linked to your bot profile, ready for monitoring and analytics without any extra effort on your part.

Step 3: Start Managing
With your group added and registered, you’re ready to manage it! Access the bot’s features like real-time updates, ticket management, and analytics directly from Telegram. Use simple commands to view group activity, approve join requests, or analyze performance metrics. @webhookos_bot makes it easy to keep everything organized, saving you time and effort while you focus on growing your corporate presence.

How to Use:

1. Go to your group "settings".
2. Tap "Add members".
3. Сlick on the plus to add @webhookos_bot!

Features

Unlock powerful tools with @webhookos_bot!

Monitoring
Keep a close eye on your corporate groups and teams with automatic tracking. @webhookos_bot monitors activity in real-time, providing updates on member joins, post views, and group status changes. Whether you’re managing a single team or multiple departments, this feature ensures you’re always informed about what’s happening without needing to check manually. Stay proactive and responsive with minimal effort!

Tickets
Streamline membership management with our intuitive ticket system. When someone requests to join your group, @webhookos_bot creates a ticket with details like the user’s ID, join time, and expiration. You can approve, reject, or close these requests directly from Telegram, keeping your corporate community secure and organized. Perfect for managing large teams or exclusive partnerships!

Analytics
Gain valuable insights into your group’s performance with detailed analytics. @webhookos_bot tracks metrics like join rates, post views, member activity, and more, giving you a clear picture of your community’s health. Use this data to optimize strategies, measure engagement, and plan growth—all from within Telegram. Turn raw numbers into actionable decisions with ease!
